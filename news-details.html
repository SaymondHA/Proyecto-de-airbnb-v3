Welcome to the new and luxurious in sanjose

San José is the capital of Costa Rica and the head of the homonymous region. Located in the center of the country, in the Central Valley, it is the most important political, economic and social center of the country. Here, the construction of real estate has not stopped growing in recent years and, due to the increase in permits to build in height, the condominiums that house luxury apartments are getting taller, especially in the west, in areas such as Rohrmoser, Pavas (which hosts one of the four international airports in Costa Rica) and the surroundings of La Sabana Metropolitan Park, one of the many green areas in the city. San José has the best basic services such as transportation, education and health, and offers a cool and pleasant climate, a diverse cultural and nightlife, and even one of the largest shopping centers in Central America. .

The Old Town has a multitude of historic buildings featuring European Neoclassical and Baroque architecture. The Mata Redonda district (in La Sabana) is one of the most expensive in the city, where you can find beautiful luxury apartments. San José is part of the Greater Metropolitan Area of ​​Costa Rica (GAM), one of the 7 planning regions in which the country is divided, the most economically active and which concentrates the vast majority of the population. The GAM also includes neighboring areas to the city of San José, such as Curridabat and San Pedro, to the east, where it is possible to buy a home in exclusive residences or condominiums. But it is to the west of the city where most of the luxury real estate market is concentrated: in San Antonio, Escazú or Santa Ana you can find wonderful luxury houses in a quiet environment but with all the best services. The most beautiful nature, in short, joins the best of the city, such as excellent hotels, shops and high-quality restaurants, office areas, golf courses, in a place with a clearly profitable real estate investment.

Costa Rica is one of the safest countries in Latin America with the highest quality of life, an earthly paradise with beaches, mountains, a warm climate and an important luxury real estate market in old buildings and modern properties. It is also a good country to invest in since its development promises great profitability in the near future. In the Central Valley you will find the largest supply of luxury homes, such as in Escazú and Santa Ana, two of the largest and most well-known cities in Costa Rica. Buying or renting an apartment in Santa Ana, where construction has grown a lot in recent years, allows you to have a large number of services: sports, cultural, .

Commercial and educational centers, restaurants, clinics and common areas with high security systems and good transport links. Other interesting places in the Central Valley where the offer of high standing is abundant are: Alajuela, Ciudad Colón, Pinares, Curridabat, Atenas and Colinas de Heredia. If what you want is to buy a luxury office, it is convenient to look in the west of San José, an area with many business hotels and that has experienced a large increase in the working population, or in Heredia, a strategic area for being very close to the best business and residential complexes, important luxury hotels and the Juan Santamaría International Airport.

Very close to San José is Cartago, a good option to combine work and rest, investing in agricultural land and resting in a villa with spectacular views of the mountains. In the city you can find luxury homes in colonial or modern style. For investors interested in tourism or a second home, the coastal provinces are interesting: Guanacaste and Puntarenas, on the Pacific, and Limón, on the Caribbean Sea. In Jacó, on the Pacific coast, volcanic sand beaches meet a lush tropical jungle. Jaco Beach, a surfer's paradise, is home to refined condominiums. This small but charming city also offers a lively nightlife. Other interesting locations on the coast are Tamarindo, Quepos, Manuel Antonio, Los Sueños and Domincal.
